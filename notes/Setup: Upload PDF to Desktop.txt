How to upload a pdf to the desktop

curl -X POST http://{dektop_ip}:5000/api/upload \
     -F "pdf=@{/path/to/pdf.pdf}"

- Replace {desktop_ip} with the ip address of the desktop
- Replace {path/to/pdf.pdf} with your pdf to upload

this will upload it to the 'data/papers' directory on the desktop for querying


example: 

curl -X POST http://{localhost}:5000/api/upload \
     -F "pdf=@/Users/richiewhite/Desktop/VGG16.pdf"
